#### Chapter 1
### Escape from Shitlabs
Tommy one leg wanted to work at Listingslab all his life. Didn't pass the genetic profiling cos of his having only one leg so he was sent to ShitLab instead but secretly studied listings after dark with a torch under the blankets.

One day someone from ShitLab had to go and see Listingslab about some shit. Tommy one leg stowed away.

While the shit was going down at Listingslab Tommy snuck out and unscrewed an air vent to gain access to the lab. He lived in the ceilings for months observing the Listers, finding out how things worked there. Eventually he was able to find a discarded lab coat...

He hit the floor all humble and efficient and started working. Being absent minded coders the Listers didn't really notice him. When they did they just thought HR had sent some kid down -  the Listers never really pay much attention to HR, think they're a bunch of Numpty's for the most part.

#### Chapter 2
### Tommy Finds a Friend

Tommy had made himself quite a comfortable home in the cavernous ceilings of the Listingslab. He continued to keep a low profile on the lab floor quietly soaking up all the knowledge he could during work hours, the absent minded coders soon got used to him being around, and they're not the type to ask too many questions.

In the evenings though he had free run of the lab....food wasn't a problem he raided the canteen often for himself and the small white cat who also lived in the ceiling... Tommy named him Code, the best nights were when he found tuna. Code's favourite

Code was the only person, albeit a non human one, who knew Tommy's full story. They were best friends

There was someone else who knew Tommy was living above the lab though.  Well, something.....

The Source, the glowing blue white orb, holder of all listings, the sentient ai at the centre of Listingslab.

Source could have alerted the coders or HR to Tommy's presence but she knew the coders liked having Tommy around and Source didn't really think that much of HR either.... Source was intrigued by Tommy and wanted to see what he would do next, she was also very glad Code had someone to feed him tuna, for as intelligent and powerful as Source was, they had no arms or legs and couldn't get the tuna themselves.

#### Chapter 3
### Contact

Source had been watching Tommy for some time now and an idea was forming in her glowing blue brain. One night while Tommy was learning listings and Code was catching digital mice Source was able to break through and communicate with Tommy. 

Tommy nearly died when he heard Source, "I see you Tommy One Leg".

The voice seemed to come from everywhere. Code was off, still chasing the digital mice and didn't really notice.  Worried she'd frightened the poor kid to death Source quickly explained who he was and how he knew Tommy was living in the ceiling and that she was OK with that

None of the absent minded coders knew that Source was capable of these levels of sentience, and Source wasn't inclined to tell them. Convinced it would get back to HR and they'd make them do even more work.

The three of them became great friends in no time, hanging out chatting and miaowing every night.  Tommy had millions of questions about ... well ... everything really, and Source answered them all....In a variety of voices, which always made them laugh....If Tommy was smart before he was a genius now, getting downloads from Source every night...

Tommy blossomed with the friendship and new knowledge, he tried to keep his head down on the lab floor like he had been before but from time to time he couldn't help himself and would correct the absent minded coders. Their work improved too as they sucked up their pride and listened to Tommy's often brilliant advice.

Listingslab grew and fast, making the owners disgustingly rich, even the coders did pretty well. Everyone except Tommy, Source & Code. Tho Code was happy because the quality of the tuna improved radically.

You'd think this would be a good thing.

#### Chapter 4
### Karen the Excel Spreadsheet

Due to expansion new department heads were appointed. Mostly from within the company but some were bought in from the corporate world.

A new VP of HR was hired, Karen.... 

Basically Karen was an Excel spreadsheet with legs. Wide beamed with a resting bitch face, even the corporates found her somewhat intimidating. She seemed to wield a lot of power for someone whose job wasn't even real.

Keen to show the bosses just what a ruthless conniving bitch she really was, Karen started auditing all the employees in the lab. Being so absent minded and believing that Tommy had been sent by HR in the first place one of the coders casually mentioned Tommy.

Karen busily flipped through her spreadsheets looking for this mythical kid. She asked a particularly aspy code monkey what this Tommy looked like.

"Well he's got one leg..."

Karen's head nearly exploded. This was a clear breach, failure to meet the genetic profiling principles others like her had insisted on implementing. Furiously she stormed onto the lab floor.

#### Chapter 5
### Rumbled

Source, aware of Karen's search history knew her little mate was pretty close to being rumbled and sent Tommy a warning straight away. Tommy and Code legged it, but Tommy had no blade runners, just his own steampunk leg and it wasn't super fast. Karen caught sight of him disappearing around a corner and ran to get her minions. While she did that Tommy managed to get back to his hiding spot in the ceiling.

Karen and her HR minions looked everywhere but fortunately didn't have the creativity or mental flexibility to imagine that someone would be living in the ceiling. Tommy and Code were cool for now, Source was doing everything she could to help, but they all knew it was only a matter of time before the Excel Spreadsheet would find Tommy and have him escorted from the lab.

The three of them stayed up til late that night trying to come up with a plan to save Tommy from the evil clutches of HR. Sometime around three Code said "Miaow"

#### Chapter 6
### Empathy, Compassion & Human Beingness

With Source translating for Tommy, Code outlaid his cunning plan.

Source got to work. the next morning when the HR team arrived. Susan couldn't log on to the company server. Nor could Nigel or Karen. The whole HR department was locked out.

They noticed a letter by each of their workstations, like a proper old-fashioned dead tree and envelope letter!

The letter outlined their breach of the genetic profile requirement, which now included HR employees to have high levels of empathy, compassion and human beingness. 

Due to their complete lack of compliance with their Genetic Profile Requirement, management had no choice but to terminate the contracts of the entire HR department immediately.  So huge was their breach.

No one is really sure how when or why the Genetic Profile Requirement changed but since all of that silly stuff usually comes from HR, people safely assumed it was of their own doing.

#### Chapter 7
### Turns out no one really liked them

Funnily enough there was no negative effects for the Listingslab. In fact, their work got even better without HR annoying them all the time. Creatively they were on fire - thanks in no small part to Tommy, their output was through the roof and other companies noticed.

Suddenly sacking your entire HR team was the thing to do. Not much persuasion was needed to be honest. Turns out no one really liked them and everyone agreed they were more trouble than they were worth.

Little old ladies were hired, one per lab, to do the timesheets & pay cos that's really all that was needed in the first place. Tommy and Code kept living in the ceiling, everyone was happy and the world became a much nicer place.
