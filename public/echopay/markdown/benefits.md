> Virtual Dashboard - No more reading out card numbers on the phone

### Pay-by-Link 

Pay-by-Link has been made even more straightforward with our new card tokenisation process. Stored encrypted in the cloud, our tokenisation allows for the customer card details to be saved on your account, enabling seamless 2-click payments. No more reading out card numbers on the phone!

### Web based dashboard

Our virtual terminal allows businesses to securely process payments from any device with an internet connection—no physical card reader required.

Perfect for phone, mail, or remote orders, it's a simple web-based interface to enter customer payment details, process transactions in real time, and generate receipts. 

### Employees System

One subscription for your entire accounts team. Our employee system allows you to create multiple logins for your entire staff, with different levels of access that you can tailor to their seniority.

### Enhanced Reporting 

We've updated our financial reporting processes, allowing you to search payment links by account number, payment references, and amount. It's also easy to export payments to CSVs on a day-by-day basis.
