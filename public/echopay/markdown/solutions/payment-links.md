### Generate a New Link
We will give you access to a portal that enables you to generate payment links to send via email, text, or WhatsApp. Each link is customised by selecting a branch (if applicable), the customer's account number/invoice number and the amount to pay.

### Send Payment Request
We will design you a HTML email that you can use to send payment requests to your customer with. Simply generate a link and hyperlink it in the email template. For text and WhatsApp you can simply add your own message and paste the link in.
