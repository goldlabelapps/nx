### With a prescription

Medical cannabis is legal in the UK if prescribed by a specialist doctor. Patients can legally purchase medical cannabis from licensed pharmacies or clinics, including some online services, provided they have a valid prescription. Importing prescribed medical cannabis from abroad is only allowed with the appropriate Home Office import license.
