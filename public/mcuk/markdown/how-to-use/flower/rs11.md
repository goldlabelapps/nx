RS11, sometimes referred to as “Rainbow Sherbet #11,” is celebrated for its balanced, uplifting head high and mellow body relaxation. Expect a sweet, fruity aroma layered with subtle peppery or earthy notes. This strain is known to boost creativity and sociability, making it an excellent choice for friendly get-togethers or light daytime activities. (Vegan-friendly dried flower.)

What you will love: sherbet and citrus that tastes premium, smooth even burn, tidy grind and a calm but social finish when using the RS11 strain.

What you can expect: a relaxed, friendly vibe that softens the body without heavy couch lock so plans still happen, a marker of RS11 enjoyment.

Why pick this one: much talked about lineage reported as Pink Guava x OZK in many cuts, and praised for flavour that stands out in a session with RS11.

The essentials: Type: indica leaning hybrid • Format: dried flower • THC level: typically high teens to mid twenties by dry weight, varies by batch • CBD: typically low • Common terpenes: limonene, myrcene, beta caryophyllene, with linalool sometimes present • Packaging: resealable pouch • Dietary: vegan friendly.
