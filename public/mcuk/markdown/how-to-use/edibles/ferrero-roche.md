#### Wellbeing Notes & Responsible Use

Comfort and calmer nights, dessert style. Non-inhaled cannabinoids can show on-average improvements for pain and sleep for some adults compared with placebo, with responses that vary person to person. That is why portion control matters.

Truffles are easy to quarter so you can keep it small, wait, then decide. This is a retail edible, not a licensed medicine, but the evening-first rhythm and slow oral route are exactly what many choose when they want a sweet finish that builds gently.


#### Clarity that helps you portion

Total per pack: 2 x 50 mg truffles, 100 mg total. If you are new, begin with ¼ truffle which is about 12.5 mg, then wait 60 to 120 minutes before you add more. Experienced users often enjoy ½ truffle which is about 25 mg after dinner. How will it feel? A gradual, dessert-paced lift that settles into a soft coast. Driving? Do not. Plan a home-first evening.
