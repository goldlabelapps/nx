## What is a Ting?

Every time a visitor interacts with your app (whether logged in or not), a "ting" document is created or updated in the Firestore `tings` collection. This keeps your app in sync with user activity and events, enabling personalized experiences and timely support. With this setup, you can focus on building relationships and delivering value, while the platform takes care of the technical details behind the scenes. Talk to your app users _when_ they are using the app. No AI, no robots. Just be there to help them through the sales process. Every visitor (authenticated or not) continuously creates or updates a ting document in the Firestore collecttion `tings`.

> Tings let you engage with your users in real time, right when they’re using your app—no bots, no AI, just authentic, human support throughout the sales process.
