Discovery focused on how work actually happened inside the firm, not how it was described.

This phase included:
- Stakeholder interviews
- Workflow mapping
- Identification of repetitive cognitive tasks
- Explicit identification of areas where AI must not be used

The outcome was a narrow, defensible problem statement rather than a broad AI mandate.
