The client operates in a highly regulated German legal environment with strict expectations around confidentiality, auditability, and professional liability.

Any technical solution had to respect:
- Legal privilege
- German data protection norms
- Conservative risk tolerance
- Non-technical users with zero appetite for experimentation

This context defined every technical decision that followed.
