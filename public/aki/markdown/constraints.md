The project was defined more by constraints than features.

Key non-negotiables:
- No data leaves the firm’s control
- No third-party model training on client data
- Full isolation from public AI services
- Predictable behaviour over maximum capability
- Clear audit boundaries

These constraints ruled out most off-the-shelf AI products.
