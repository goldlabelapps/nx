> Ace your software developer interviews. Master technical interview communication, behavioral questions, system design explanations, and English interview skills.

> Coding Interviews  

_What_: Solve algorithmic problems while explaining your approach  
_Duration_: 45-60 minutes  
_Key Skills_: Problem-solving, code quality, communication. 

> System Design Interviews  

_What_: Design scalable systems (e.g., "Design Twitter")  
_Duration_: 45-90 minutes. 
_Key Skills_: Architecture knowledge, tradeoff discussion, whiteboarding  

> Behavioral Interviews  

_What_: Discuss past experiences and scenarios.  
_Duration_: 30-45 minutes.  
_Key Skills_: Storytelling, self-awareness, cultural fit  

> Technical Deep Dives  

_What_: Detailed discussion of past projects  
_Duration_: 30-60 minutes  
_Key Skills_: Technical depth, decision explanation, reflection
